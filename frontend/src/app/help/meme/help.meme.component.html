Meme help

Title

  • The title should help you recall what the image is/looks like. For memes, the first part of the meme text may be suitable.

Caption

  • A caption is pithy/funny/sarcastic text that people can share along with the meme. A good caption can make a huge difference in how well received your meme is.
  • You and other people can add additional captions or comment to the meme later.
  • Optional - limit of 280 characters

Tag notes

  • Tags will automatically be prefixed with # when tweeting, so don't include #
  • Use PascalCase for tags with two or more words (e.g. AlphaBeta - NOT: alphaBeta, alpha_beta, alpha beta)
  • Use underscores (_) for subcategories. e.g. Muslims_Terrorism. Try to avoid subcategories though - use only when number of images grows beyond 50 for the main category.
  • Prefer plural over singular: e.g. Communists rather Communist
  • camelCase is reserved for special memes used to troll, argue, etc. (troll, rhetoric...)
  • Use "auspol" (without quotes) for Aussie content. Most memes aren't specific to Australia.: use #auspol for the ones that are
  • Use "ALP" for Labor party
  • Use "LNP" for Liberal party. "Liberals" refer to American liberals (Democrats).
