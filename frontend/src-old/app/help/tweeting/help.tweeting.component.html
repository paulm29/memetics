Tweeting help

Tweeting notes

  • Don't get yourself shadow-banned! Shadow banning is when you and your followers can see your tweets, but the rest of the world can't. Shadow banning can occur when you tweet too frequently to a hashtag (i.e. exceed rate limit) using the same text content (including hashtags). We can't say exactly how you can be shadowbanned, because Twitter doesn't want people to know so that spambots can't find workarounds. Ideally, always include some varied text when you tweet a meme, and give it at least a minute between tweets. Shadow bans may be temporary, but nothing is worse than finding out very few people are seeing your tweets due to rate limits.
